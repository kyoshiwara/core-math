Solution 1.2.4.1.

First we'll find \(\dfrac{1}{10}~\) of 3200.

\begin{equation*} \dfrac{1}{10}~~ \text{of}~~ 3200 = 3200 \div 10 = 320 \end{equation*}

To find 0.4, or \(\dfrac{4}{10}\text{,}\) of 3200, we can multiply \(\dfrac{1}{10}\) of 3200 by 4.

\begin{equation*} \dfrac{4}{10}~~ \text{of}~~ 3200 = 320 \times 4 = 1280 \end{equation*}

1280 students take the bus.
