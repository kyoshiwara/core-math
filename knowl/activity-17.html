Activity 2.2.1. Percents.

Follow the steps to find each percent. Try to do the calculations in your head!
  1. The Kwans are buying a house for $340,000, and they want to make a down payment of 20% of the price.
    1. What is 10% of the price of the house?
    2. What is 20% of the price of the house?
  2. The restaurant bill came to $26, and Tyrell wants to leave a 15% tip.
    1. What is 10% of the bill?
    2. What is 5% of the bill?
    3. What is 15% of the bill?
  3. The sales tax on a $25 rose bush is 8%.
    1. What is 10% of the price of the rose bush?
    2. What is 1% of the price of the rose bush?
    3. What is 8% of the price of the rose bush?
  4. In 2020 there were approximately 72 million children under the age of 18 in the United States. About 16% of them lived in poverty.
    1. What was 10% of the US population of children?
    2. What was 5% of the US population of children?
    3. What was 1% of the US population of children?
    4. What was 16% of the US population of children?
