Activity 4.2.2. Dividing Fractions.

For Problems 1-6,

  1. Divide fractions.

  2. Explain what each division means, and illustrate with rectangles.

  1. \(\displaystyle 4 \div \dfrac{1}{4}\)

  2. \(\displaystyle 3 \div \dfrac{1}{6}\)

  3. \(\displaystyle 4 \div \dfrac{2}{3}\)

  4. \(\displaystyle 6 \div \dfrac{3}{4}\)

  5. \(\displaystyle 1\dfrac{3}{4} \div \dfrac{7}{8}\)

  6. \(\displaystyle 2\dfrac{1}{2} \div \dfrac{5}{6}\)
