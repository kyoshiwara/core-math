Solution 3.3.1.1.

  1. There are 12 inches in a foot, so 5 feet is equal to
    \begin{equation*} 5 \times 12 = 60~~\text{inches} \end{equation*}
    and 5 feet 8 inches is equal to
    \begin{equation*} 60 + 8 = 68~~\text{inches} \end{equation*}
    Rihanna is 68 inches tall.
  2. What fraction of a foot is 8 inches?
    \begin{equation*} \dfrac{8}{12} = \dfrac{2}{3}~~\text{foot} \end{equation*}
    Rihanna is \(5 \dfrac{2}{3}\) feet tall.
