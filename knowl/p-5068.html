To divide \(\dfrac{15}{4}\) by \(\dfrac{3}{8}\text{,}\) we multiply \(\dfrac{15}{4}\) by \(\dfrac{8}{3}\text{.}\)

\begin{align*} \dfrac{15}{4} \div \dfrac{3}{8} \amp = \dfrac{15}{4} \times \dfrac{8}{3} = \dfrac{15 \times 8}{4 \times 3} ~~~~~~ \blert{\dfrac{\text{Multiply numerators together.}}{\text{Multiply denominators together.}}}\\ \amp = \dfrac{120}{12} = 10 \end{align*}
