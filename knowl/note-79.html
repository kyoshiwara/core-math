Note 4.5.17.

Be careful! In Exercise 3, did you add 9 + 16 to get 25 before you took the square root? Look at Step 1 of the order of operations: Perform any operations ... or inside a radical. In other words,
\begin{equation*} \sqrt{9+16} \hphantom{0000} \text{is not equal to} \hphantom{0000} \sqrt{9} + \sqrt{16} \end{equation*}
