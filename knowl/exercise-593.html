Exercise 1.

Rachel is eight years older than Simone.

Simone's age 6 10 15 30
Rachel's age 14 26 32

  1. Explain how to find Rachel's age when you know Simone's age.

  2. Explain how to find Simone's age when you know Rachel's age.

  3. Let \(r\) stand for Rachel's age. Write an equation for \(r\) in terms of Simone's age, \(s\text{.}\)

  4. How can you find \(r\) if you know \(s\text{?}\)

  5. How can you find \(s\) if you know \(r\text{?}\)
