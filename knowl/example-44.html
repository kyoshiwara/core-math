Example 2.3.4.

  1. In the table below, do you see a pattern that relates the total utility bill to your share?
    Utility Bill ($) Your Share ($)
    28 7
    36 9
    42 10.50
    Yes: Your share is one fourth of the total bill, so you can divide the total bill by 4 to find your share.
  2. Write a mathematical sentence that expresses your share of the bill in terms of the total bill.
    \begin{equation*} \text{Your share} = \blert{\text{utility bill} \div 4} \end{equation*}
