Activity 1.5.2. Area.

In this Activity, all grids represent centimeters.
  1. Estimate the area of each region by piecing together squares as necessary.
  2. Use the fact that a triangle is half of a rectangle to find the area of each region. Hint: It may help to draw in a useful vertical line (or two).
  3. Find the area of each region by enclosing it in a rectangle and subtracting the areas of the parts outside the region. Show your method.
