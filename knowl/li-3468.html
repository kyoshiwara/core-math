Item.

The scale factor is the ratio of the lengths in the new figure to the corresponding lengths in the original figure. We can write this relationship as a mathematical sentence as follows:
\begin{equation*} \text{scale factor} = \dfrac{\text{new length}}{\text{corresponding original length}} \end{equation*}
or
\begin{equation*} \text{new length} = \text{scale factor} \times \text{corresponding original length} \end{equation*}
