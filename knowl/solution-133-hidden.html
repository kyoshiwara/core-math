We will check to see whether the ratio \(\dfrac{q}{p}\) is constant.
\(~~\vphantom{\dfrac{1}{2}} p~~\) 8 20 28 52
\(\vphantom{\dfrac{1}{2}} q\) 2 5 7 13
\(\vphantom{\dfrac{1}{2}} \dfrac{q}{p}\) \(\dfrac{2}{8} = 0.25\) \(\dfrac{5}{20} = 0.25\) \(\dfrac{7}{28} = 0.25\) \(\dfrac{13}{52} = 0.25\)
Yes, \(\dfrac{q}{p} = 0.25\text{,}\) so \(q\) is proportional to \(p\text{.}\)
