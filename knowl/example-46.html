Example 2.3.8.

In the 1990’s, when cell phones first became available, Motorola and Nokia were competing phone companies. The bar graph shows the portion of the market captured by each company from 1994 to 2000.
  1. What was Motorola’s share of the market in 2000? \(~~~~~~\blert{\text{15%}}\)
  2. In which year shown did Motorola have a 24% market share? \(~~~~~~\blert{\text{1997}}\)
  3. In which of the years shown did Motorola have its largest market share? What was that share?
    \begin{equation*} \blert{\text{In 1994 Motorola had 33% of the market.}} \end{equation*}
  4. By how much did Motorola’s market share exceed Nokia’s in 1995?
    \begin{equation*} \blert{\text{8 percentage points}} \end{equation*}
  5. In which of the years shown did Motorola have its largest lead over Nokia in market share? What was that lead?
    \begin{equation*} \blert{\text{In 1994 Motorola had a 12% lead in market share.}} \end{equation*}
