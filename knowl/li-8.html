Item b.
\({\dfrac{1}{2}}\) of an hour (60 minutes) \(=\) 30 minutes
\(~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~\blert{\text{A whole hour is 60 minutes.}~~~~~~~~~~\text{Our part is 30 minutes:} ~~\dfrac{30}{60}}\)
\begin{equation*} \frac{\text{part}}{\text{whole}} = \large{\dfrac{30}{60}} = \large{\dfrac{1}{2}}~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~ \end{equation*}
