Item 5.

The last four columns in each table are for recording some calculations. You should be able to interpret what goes in each column:

\((FL)^2\text{:}\) Square the length of the first leg.

\((SL)^2\text{:}\) Square the length of the second leg.

\((FL)^2 + (SL)^2\text{:}\) Add the entries in the previous two columns.

\((H)^2\text{:}\) Square the length of the hypotenuse.
