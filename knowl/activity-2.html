Activity 1.1.2. Fourths.

Show your work for each calculation.

  1. Find one-fourth of the amount by taking one-half of one-half.

  2. Find three-fourths of the amount by subtracting one-fourth from the whole.

  3. Find three-fourths of the amount by multiplying one-fourth by three. Are your answers to parts (b) and (c) the same?

  1. Norman's monthly salary is $3600. He deposits one-fourth of his salary in his savings account, and the rest in his checking account.

  2. Jaycee owns a ranch of 3000 acres. She plans to grow corn on one-fourth of her land and raise cattle on the rest.

  3. Rich is on a diet that allows 2100 calories per day. He consumes one-quarter of his calories at breakfast, and the rest throughout the day.

  4. Caroline drove one-quarter of a 900-mile trip today. How far did she drive, and how far does she have left to go?
