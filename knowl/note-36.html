Note 2.4.7.

Sometimes English phrases can be tricky. We must be especially careful with subtraction and division.

  • In Example 3a, the expression \(16 - z\) is not a correct answer.

  • In Example 3b, "\(m\) divided by 6" means \(\dfrac{m}{6}\text{,}\) but "\(m\) divided into 6" means \(\dfrac{6}{m}\text{.}\)
