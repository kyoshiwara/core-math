Item b.

\({\dfrac{3}{4}}\) of an hour (60 minutes) \(=\) 45 minutes

\(~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~\blert{\text{A whole hour is 60 minutes.}~~~~~~~~\text{Our part is 45 minutes:} ~~\dfrac{45}{60}}\)
\begin{equation*} \frac{\text{part}}{\text{whole}} = \large{\dfrac{45}{60}} = \large{\dfrac{3}{4}}~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~ \end{equation*}
