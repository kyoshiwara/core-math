Exercise Group 2.4.8.53–56.

For Problems 53-56,
  1. Write an algebraic expression.
  2. Evaluate the expression.
53.
  1. Amanda’s bread recipe calls for three cups of flour more than she has. Choose a variable for the amount of flour Amanda has, then write an expression for the amount of flour required.
  2. If Amanda has 8 cups of flour, how much flour does the recipe call for?
54.
  1. Garth received 432 fewer votes than his opponent in the election. Choose a variable for the number of votes Garth’s opponent received, then write an expression for the number of votes Garth received.
  2. If Garth’s opponent got 3297 votes, how many votes did Garth get?
55.
  1. To find his homework grade, Jamal should divide his total points by 600. Choose a variable for Jamal’s total points, then write an expression for Jamal’s homework grade.
  2. If Jamal earned 480 homework points, what is his homework grade?
56.
  1. Each member of the Forensics Club pays $5 in dues. Choose a variable for the number of members, then write an expression for the amount the club earns in dues.
  2. If the Forensics Club has 38 members, how much does it earn in dues?
