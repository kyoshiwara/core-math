Exercise 26.
Joshua is saving newspapers for the paper drive at his son's school. The graph shows the amount of newspaper \(P\) he has saved \(t\) weeks after the paper drive began.
  1. How many pounds of newspaper has Joshua saved after 4 weeks?
  2. When will Joshua have saved 180 pounds of newspapers?
  3. Compute the slope of the graph, \(\dfrac{\Delta P}{\Delta t}\text{,}\) including units.
  4. What does the slope tell you about the problem?
