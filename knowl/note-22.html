Note 2.1.2.

Be careful when reading decimal fractions! Remember that the position or place value of a digit tells us the denominator of the fraction.

\begin{align*} 0.4~~~~~~ \amp \text{is four tenths} \amp\amp \text{or}~~~~~\dfrac{4}{10}\\ 0.04~~~~~~ \amp \text{is four hundredths} \amp\amp \text{or}~~~~~\dfrac{4}{100}\\ 0.004~~~~~~ \amp \text{is four thousandths} \amp\amp \text{or}~~~~~\dfrac{4}{1000} \end{align*}

In the same way, 4 and 40 and 400 are very different numbers.
