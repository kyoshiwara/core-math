From week 2 to week 6, the vine grew at a rate of
\begin{align*} \dfrac{\Delta h}{\Delta t} \amp = \dfrac{\text{increase in height}}{\text{time interval}}\\ \amp = \dfrac{(26-10)~\text{inches}}{(6-2)~\text{weeks}}\\ \amp = \dfrac{\text{16 inches}}{\text{4 weeks}} = \text{4 inches/week} \end{align*}
