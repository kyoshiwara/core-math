Activity 6.1.1. Comparing Quantities.

Decide whether additive or multiplicative comparison is appropriate.
  • If the relationship is additive, write a difference and a sentence to describe it.
  • If the relationship is multiplicative, write a quotient and a sentence to describe it.
  1. Kelli and Kendra swim at the same speed, but Kelli swam 4 laps before Kendra started. Compare the number of laps the girls have completed after half an hour.
  2. David and Dana signed up to bake brownies for a charity event. David says he will bake four dozen brownies, and Dana will bake three dozen. Compare the amounts of each ingredient they will need.
