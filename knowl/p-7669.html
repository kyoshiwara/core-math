The notion of rate of increase can be applied to other equations besides proportions. Recall that the graph of a proportion is a straight line that passes through the point \((0,0)\text{.}\) But as long as the graph is a straight line we can still talk about its rate of increase, even if it does not pass through the point \((0,0)\text{.}\) In the next example we'll compare the rate of growth of two strains of tomato plants.
