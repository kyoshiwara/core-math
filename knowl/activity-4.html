Activity 1.2.1. One-Tenth.

Decide whether the fraction is more or less than one-tenth. Show how you decided.
  1. The tax on a $68 purchase was $5.78. Is that more or less than one-tenth of the price?
  2. Randall has plowed 11 acres of his 80-acre corn field. Is that more or less than one-tenth of the field?
  3. The Wildlife Conservancy tagged 23 of the 312 geese at Blue Lake. Is that more or less than one-tenth of the geese?
  4. Gregor paid $4500 towards his $30,000 student loan. Is that more or less than one-tenth of the amount he owes?
