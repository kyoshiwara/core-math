Activity 1.2.2. Tenths.

Find the fraction. Show your work.
  1. A new section of county road will be 18 miles long. The road crew has completed 0.3 of the road. How long is the completed portion?
  2. The average annual rainfall in Salem County is 39 inches. This year only 0.8 of that amount of rain fell. How many inches of rain fell this year?
  3. Caroline spends $2400 a month, or 0.6 of her income, on food and rent.
    1. What is one-tenth of Caroline's monthly income?
    2. What is Caroline's monthly income?
  4. Radio station KWHY collected $4760 in the first week of their 10-day pledge drive. If they maintain the same level of support, how much can they expect to make by the end of the pledge drive?
