Exercise 3.

Delbert's checking account has an outstanding check (not yet received by the bank) for $76. So his actual balance is $76 less than the balance shown on his bank statement.

Bank statement ($) 100 138 188 332
Actual balance ($) 24 200 276

  1. Explain how to find the actual balance from the bank statement.

  2. Explain how to find the bank's figure for the balance if you know the actual balance.

  3. Let \(a\) stand for the actual balance. Write an equation for \(a\) in terms of the bank statement, \(b\text{.}\)

  4. How can you find \(a\) if you know \(b\text{?}\)

  5. How can you find \(b\) if you know \(a\text{?}\)
