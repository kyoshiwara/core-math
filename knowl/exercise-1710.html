Exercise 13.

For every three sailboats registered to use the lake, there are four motorboats.
  1. What is the ratio of sailboats to motorboats?
  2. What is the ratio of motorboats to sailboats?
  3. What fraction of all the boats registered are sailboats? What fraction are motorboats?
  4. If there are 36 sailboats registered, how many motorboats are registered?
  5. If there are 60 motorboats registered, how many sailboats are registered?
