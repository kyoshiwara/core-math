Activity 1.3.3. The Sieve of Eratosthenes.

  1. Circle the first prime number, 2. Cross out all multiples of 2.
  2. Circle the first number that is not crossed out. (It should be a prime number.) Cross out all multiples of that number.
  3. Repeat part (b) until there are no more numbers to cross out.
The circled numbers are the primes!
