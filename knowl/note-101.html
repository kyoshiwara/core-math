Note 7.1.11.

Here is an informal strategy for adding signed numbers:
Step 1 Do the two numbers have the same sign or opposite signs?
Step 2a (Same sign) Great: add 'em up and keep the same sign.
Step 2b (Opposite signs) Which number is "bigger" (farther from zero)? That number wins -- use the sign of that number. Subtract to see how much bigger.
