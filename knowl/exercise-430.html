Exercise 22.

At the census of 2010, the population of the U.S. was 308,745,000. In 2020, the population was 331,449,000.
  1. What was the increase in population over that decade?
  2. By what percent of the 2010 population did the population increase from 2010 to 2020?
  3. If the population continues to increase by the same percent, what will the U.S. population be in 2030?
