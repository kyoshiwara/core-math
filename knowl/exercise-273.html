Exercise 2.

On another grid, copy the three rectangles you drew in problem 1, but arrange them into one composite (combined) shape.

  1. Do you think the area of the new shape will be larger, smaller, or the same as the sum of the areas of the three rectangles? Calculate the area of the new shape to find out.

  2. Do you think the perimeter of the new shape you made in problem 2 will be larger, smaller, or the same as the sum of the perimeters of the three rectangles? Calculate the perimeter of the new shape to find out.
