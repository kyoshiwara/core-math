What is one-tenth of one-tenth?
One-tenth of one-tenth is one-hundredth, or \(\dfrac{1}{100}~\text{.}\) In decimal form, we write \(\dfrac{1}{100}~\) as 0.01, with the 1 in the second decimal place, or hundredths place.
\begin{equation*} \blert{\text{one-hundredth} = \dfrac{1}{100} = 0.01} \end{equation*}
