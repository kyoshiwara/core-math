Activity 1.1.1.

Write a fraction for each story, then decide whether the fraction is:

  1. equal to one-half,

  2. less than one-half, or

  3. more than one-half.

  1. Greta used a yardstick to measure a picture frame that is 20 inches wide. What fraction of a yard is that? (A yard is 36 inches.)
