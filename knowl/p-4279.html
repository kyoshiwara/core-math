The diameter of the skating rink was 50 feet, so its radius is half that, or 25 feet. We substitute \(\alert{25}\) for \(r\) in the formula for area.
\begin{align*} \text{Area } \amp = \pi \times (\alert{25})^2 \hphantom{000000} \blert{\text{Compute the power first.}}\\ \amp = \pi \times 625 \hphantom{000000} \blert{\text{Multiply.}}\\ \amp \approx 1963.50 \end{align*}
The area of the skating rink is approximately 1963.50 square feet.
