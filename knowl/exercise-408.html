Apply the one-third rule to each situation.
  1. When a home-buyer applies for a mortgage, the monthly payments on the mortgage amount should not exceed one third of the buyer's net monthly salary. Brandon's net annual salary is $48,600. What size mortgage payment can he afford?
  2. A manager should allow herself up to one third of the available time for a task to organize the task and delegate responsibility, thus allowing her team the remaining two thirds of the time. Emily has two hours to write her weekly report. How long should her team work on the report?
  3. In scuba diving, one third of the gas supply is used for the outward journey, one third for the return journey, and one third is left as a safety reserve. Farah's cylinder holds 10 liters. How many liters are left when she starts her return journey?
  4. When mowing grass, you should never remove more than one third of the grass blade. If the mowing height on your lawn mower is 3 inches, how tall should the grass be when you mow, so that you remove one third of the grass blade?
