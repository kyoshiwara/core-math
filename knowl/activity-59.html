Activity 5.4.1. Equations with Two Operations.

Emma can order T-shirts from a catalog for $5 each. She pays a $4 handling fee on her order.
  1. Write an algebraic expression for the cost of ordering \(n\) T-shirts.
  2. Write an equation for the number of T-shirts Emma can order with $34.
  3. Describe in words how to find the number of T-shirts Emma can order with $34. Your method should have two steps.
    (Hint: After she pays the handling fee, how much money is left to pay for T-shirts?
