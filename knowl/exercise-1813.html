Exercise 28.

  2. When will Joshua have saved 180 pounds of newspapers?
  3. Compute the rabbit's rate of growth, including units.
  4. Illustrate the rate of growth, \(\dfrac{\Delta W}{\Delta t}\text{,}\) on the graph.
