Item a.
At point \(P, ~t=1\) and \(C = 8\text{,}\) which tells us that it costs $8 to rent a bicycle for 1 hour. Point \(Q\) tells us that it costs $17 to rent a bicycle for 4 hours. The slope of the line is given by
\begin{align*} \dfrac{\Delta C}{\Delta t} \amp = \dfrac{17-8}{4-1}\\ \amp = \dfrac{9~\text{dollars}}{3~\text{hours}} = \text{3 dollars/hour} \end{align*}
