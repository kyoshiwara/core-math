  • Unlike addition, in subtraction it does matter which term appears first:

  • The order of the numbers also matters in division:
    \(\alert{20 \div 5 = \dfrac{20}{5} = 4},~~~\) but \(~~~\alert{5 \div 20 = \dfrac{5}{20} = \dfrac{1}{4}}\)
