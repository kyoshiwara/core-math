In fact, we can think of any string of terms as a sum, where the \(~+~\) and \(~-~\) symbols tell us the sign of the term that follows. For example,
\begin{align*} -5 - 4 ~~~~~~ \amp \text{is the sum of} ~~ -5 ~~ \text{and} ~~ -4\\ 3a - 9 ~~~~~~ \amp \text{is the sum of} ~~~~~ 3a ~~ \text{and} ~~ -9\\ -7t + 6 ~~~~~~ \amp\text{is the sum of} ~~ -7t ~~ \text{and} ~~ +6 \end{align*}
