Exercise 4.
Francine is traveling on an express train at 65 miles per hour.
Hours traveled 1 2 3.5 6
Miles traveled 65 325 650
  1. Explain how to find the distance Francine traveled if you know how many hours she traveled.
  2. Explain how to find the number of hours Francine traveled if you know how many miles she traveled.
  3. Let \(d\) stand for the distance Francine traveled. Write an equation for \(d\) in terms of the travel time, \(t\text{.}\)
  4. How can you find \(d\) if you know \(t\text{?}\)
  5. How can you find \(t\) if you know \(d\text{?}\)
