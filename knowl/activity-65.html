Activity 6.1.2. Ratios in Decimal Form.

  1. In the 2015-2016 season, Kobe Bryant attempted 281 free throws and made 232 of them. What is the ratio of his successes to his attempts?

    2. Write your answer in the form of a common fraction.
    3. Write your answer in the form of a decimal fraction, rounding to three decimal places.
    4. Write your answer as a percent.
